  <div class="about-bio">
    <h2>The Artist</h2>
    <p class="lead">I'm a <strong>Lead Character Artist</strong> crafting memorable characters for games, collectibles, and interactive experiences.</p>
    <p>I blend technical precision with artistic intuition, transforming concepts into sculpted, game-ready assets that breathe life into virtual worlds.</p>
    <p>My work spans from stylized creatures to realistic heroes. I've shipped titles with studios worldwide, delivering characters that perform in-engine while standing as sculptures.</p>
    <p>Based in Curitiba, Brazil, I bring leadership experience to every project, elevating teams and shaping visual identity through character design.</p>
